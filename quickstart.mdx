## Prerequisites

Before starting, ensure you have:

<Callout kind="info">

- A business email address (e.g., you@yourcompany.com)
- Access to a modern web browser like Chrome or Firefox
- Basic familiarity with dashboards and reporting tools

</Callout>

## Create and Verify Your Account

Sign up in under 2 minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Signup Page" icon="globe">
    Navigate to `https://app.alchemetryx.com/signup`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Details" icon="user">
    Provide your business name, email, and create a strong password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for a verification email from Alchemetryx and click the link.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Access the Main Dashboard

Once verified, log in to explore your personalized dashboard.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Web Browser" icon="globe">
    Go to `https://app.alchemetryx.com/login`, enter your credentials, and select **Dashboard**.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Mobile App" icon="smartphone">
    Download the Alchemetryx app from the App Store or Google Play, then log in.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Complete Initial Onboarding

Follow the guided setup to connect your first data source.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect Data Source" icon="database">
    In the dashboard, click **Add Data Source** and select from options like Google Analytics, QuickBooks, or CSV upload.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Business Profile" icon="settings">
    Enter key details: industry, team size (`<50` employees), and growth goals.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Summary" icon="check-circle">
    Preview your setup and click **Finish Onboarding**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Onboarding unlocks personalized recommendations based on your business profile.
</Callout>

## Generate Your First Growth Insight Report

Create actionable insights with a simple API call or dashboard button.

<CodeGroup tabs="JavaScript,cURL">
  ```javascript
  const response = await fetch('https://api.alchemetryx.com/v1/reports/growth-insight', {
    method: 'POST',
    headers: {
      'Authorization': 'Bearer YOUR_API_KEY',
      'Content-Type': 'application/json'
    },
    body: JSON.stringify({
      businessId: 'your-business-id',
      period: 'last-30-days'
    })
  });
  const report = await response.json();
  console.log(report.insights);
  ```
  ```bash
  curl -X POST https://api.alchemetryx.com/v1/reports/growth-insight \
    -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_API_KEY" \
    -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
    -d '{
      "businessId": "your-business-id",
      "period": "last-30-days"
    }'
  ```
</CodeGroup>

From the dashboard, click **Generate Report** to view results instantly.
