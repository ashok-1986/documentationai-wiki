## Overview

Alchemetryx provides tailored growth intelligence reports for business owners.

## How to Request

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log into Dashboard">
    ````
    Open https://alchemetryx.com/dashboard
    Log in with your credentials
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to 'Growth Analysis'">
    ````
    Find 'Growth Analysis' in the left menu
    Click 'Request New Report'
    Complete short business questionnaire
    Submit the form
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive your report">
    ````
    You'll be notified by email within 2 business days
    Download the PDF from your dashboard
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
